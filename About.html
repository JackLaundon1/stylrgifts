About Us!

Hiya, our names are Rosie and Sophie! We are 13 & 14 years old, and we run the amazing jewellery business StylR! Our business is based in & around Aberdeen, and we provide beautiful hand-crafted accessories at very affordable prices! We chose to start our business during lockdown after Rosie taught Sophie to make earrings, and now we have branched out to many different accessories, such as bracelets, scrunchies and more! Choose from our wide range of designs, or give us a design of your own and we will do our best to make your item to your precise specifications (provided we have the materials!)We really enjoy making these things, and we thank our customers for supporting a local business and allowing us to share our passion for jewellery!
